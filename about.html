Our Story

I came from the other side of the table.

Before starting Featherway, I spent years working inside the traditional window industry — the in-home sales visits, the pressure tactics, the four-hour appointments designed to wear you down until you signed.

I knew the process inside and out. And I never felt good about it.

"Homeowners deserve to know what they're paying before a salesperson shows up at their door."

The reality is that most homeowners already know what they want — they just want a straight answer on what it costs. The traditional model makes that nearly impossible by design. Pricing only comes at the end of a long sales process, after you've already invested hours of your time.

Featherway was built around a simple idea: give people real pricing online, without the visit, without the pressure, and without the follow-up calls. If the price works for you, great — we'll take it from there with Pella-certified installation and a clean, professional process from start to finish.

Think of us as the knowledgeable friend in the industry — someone who'll give you honest information, a fair price, and respect your time.
